College Application Timeline

A step-by-step guide from junior year to senior year graduation.

Junior Fall

Start preparing for the SAT/ACT and research colleges you're interested in.

  • Begin researching colleges: location, programs, and campus size.
  • Take practice SAT/ACT tests to assess strengths and weaknesses.
  • Attend college fairs and virtual information sessions.
  • Maintain strong grades and participate in extracurriculars.

Junior Winter

Take the PSAT and begin brainstorming essay topics.

  • Take the PSAT for National Merit consideration.
  • Meet with your counselor to review college goals.
  • Brainstorm and outline potential college essay topics.
  • Seek leadership opportunities in extracurriculars.

Junior Spring

Take AP/IB exams and finalize your college list.

  • Take AP/IB exams to strengthen academic profile.
  • Finalize preliminary college list and identify target schools.
  • Visit campuses if possible.
  • Consider summer programs, internships, or volunteering for experience.

Senior Fall

Submit college applications and request recommendation letters.

  • Complete and submit college applications before deadlines.
  • Refine essays and supplemental materials.
  • Request letters of recommendation from teachers.
  • Maintain strong grades; colleges review senior year performance.

Senior Winter

Apply for financial aid and scholarships.

  • Submit FAFSA and/or CSS Profile.
  • Apply for scholarships and grants.
  • Check college portals for missing materials.
  • Plan campus visits if needed for final decision-making.

Senior Spring

Decide on your college, submit your deposit, and prepare for graduation!

  • Review acceptance letters and compare financial aid offers.
  • Choose your college and submit the enrollment deposit.
  • Confirm housing and orientation dates.
  • Celebrate graduation and prepare academically and personally for college.
